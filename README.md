# Rich & Spice 🍽️
> **Premium Indian Fine Dining, Chennai**

Welcome to **Rich & Spice**, Chennai's premier fine-dining sanctuary where royal heritage meets modern spice craftsmanship. Inspired by the architectural grandeur of the Chola Empire, Rich & Spice offers a culinary journey guided by authenticity, texture, and fragrance.

---

## 🏛️ Our Heritage & Philosophy
At Rich & Spice, we believe that spices are the soul of gastronomy. Our kitchen is guided by the architectural grandeur and refined tastes of historical royal kitchens:
* **Freshly Stone-Ground Spices:** Hand-ground spices prepared daily for maximum depth of flavor.
* **Clay-Oven Tandoors:** Traditional clay-oven roasts prepared at high temperatures for that perfect, natural wood-fired smokiness.
* **Sustainable Sourcing:** Ingredients like organic cardamom, saffron, and coastal pepper are sourced directly from select estates in the Western Ghats.

---

## ✨ Features & Sections

* **A Royal Welcome:** Beautiful landing area featuring a modern glassmorphic header and fluid scrolling effects.
* **Heritage Storytelling:** A deep dive into our spice-crafting history and ingredients.
* **Artisanal Menu Showcase:** A categorised menu including:
  * *Starters* (Tandoori Broccoli, Pudina Paneer Tikka, Chicken Seekh, Pepper Prawns)
  * *Mains* (Paneer Makhani, Dal Makhani, Chettinad Chicken, Mutton Rarah)
  * *Desserts* (Blueberry Cheesecake, Badam Halwa, Rasmalai, Gulab Jamun)
* **Real-time Table Reservation:** A premium interactive booking portal for booking tables.
* **Customer Testimonials & Reviews:** Verified premium guest reviews and dining feedback.
* **Interactive Map & Location:** Easily locate our sanctuary in Chennai.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
* **HTML5:** Semantic architecture for structured and accessible content.
* **Vanilla CSS3:** Custom, responsive styling featuring high-end glassmorphism, custom typography (Cormorant Garamond & Inter), and micro-animations.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** Responsive navbar toggling, custom scroll reveal observers, dynamic menu category filtering, and booking system interactive states.

## 🚀 Setup & Installation
1. Clone or download this repository.

3. For the best experience, run it using a local dev server (like VS Code Live Server).
