Our Heritage

The Art of Indian Spice Craft

At Rich & Spice, we believe that spices are the soul of gastronomy. Our kitchen is guided by the architectural grandeur and refined tastes of the Chola Empire, preparing freshly hand-ground spices in traditional wood-fired tandoor clay ovens.

Every dish is an exploration of complexity, texture, and fragrance. By sourcing organic cardamom, saffron, and coastal pepper directly from select estates in the Western Ghats, we promise an unparalleled luxury experience.
